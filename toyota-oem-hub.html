Seller Talking Points — Toyota
✉
Always Remind Dealers About "I APPROVE"
Critical Enrollment Step
Toyota has a unique enrollment step not seen in other OEMs: after enrolling in tddsprogram.com, the dealer receives a Shift Digital confirmation email and must reply "I APPROVE." If they miss this, enrollment stalls. Make this part of your handoff every time.
"Toyota enrollment requires a confirmation step through Shift Digital — I'll walk you through it."
🔒
No Migration for Existing Toyota Dealers
Important Restriction
We will NOT be migrating existing Toyota dealers at this time. This applies to new enrollments only. Do not promise migration paths to dealers already in the Toyota ecosystem — route new dealers through the standard tddsprogram.com enrollment.
🎯
EA and TIA Are the OEM-Supported Products
Product Scope
Toyota supports Expert Appraisal ($849) and Trade-In Advisor ($399) as OEM-certified products. Lead Driver appears in the portal but is not listed as an OEM-supported Toyota product — focus on EA and TIA in your conversations.
"Toyota supports Trade-In Advisor and Expert Appraisal as its OEM-certified KBB solutions."
🏛
Same Framework as Lexus — Different Portal
Program Context
Toyota and Lexus share the same OEM framework ("Amazing Digital Experience") via Shift Digital — same no-co-op structure, same dealer-paid model. Key difference: Toyota uses tddsprogram.com, Lexus uses amazingdigitalexperiences.com. Also: Toyota has the "I APPROVE" email step; confirm whether Lexus requires this too.
⚠
No Co-Op — Lead With Product Value
Co-Op
Toyota has no co-op reimbursement on any product. The sales conversation rests entirely on product value — KBB's #1 brand trust, lower cost-to-market, and acquisition capability. Lead with the 76% consumer stat and the 3.2% cost-to-market advantage.
🔐
OEM Path Required — Retail Contracting Discouraged
Compliance
Toyota dealers must use OEM-certified SKUs. Retail contracts outside the OEM path are discouraged for eligible dealers. Toyota uses hard-coded dealer tagging — changes require Shift Digital approval and development work. Always use tddsprogram.com.
"Following the Toyota OEM enrollment path ensures compliance and avoids re-work."
