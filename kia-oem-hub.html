OEM Certified Through Shift Digital
Eligibility
Kia is OEM-certified through Shift Digital as the OEM program partner. Enrollment is coordinated through Kia/Shift, then flows to KBB Ops. Dealers are not on a retail-only path — they enroll through the OEM workflow.
"Kia participates in an OEM-certified KBB program, so enrollment follows Kia's OEM process rather than a retail-only path."
⚡
Existing KBB Dealers Activate Fast
Enrollment
Dealers already on KBB ICO get OEM Appraisal added at $0 — no new retail contract, no lengthy onboarding. Shortened webinar training, minimal disruption. Lead with this for dealers already in the KBB ecosystem.
"If you're already a KBB dealer, activation is simple. If not, we'll walk you through a short contracting and training process."
⬆
EA & Dealer Boost Are Available Upgrades
Upsell
OEM Appraisal is the baseline, but Expert Appraisal and Dealer Boost are both available as upgrades on the Kia program. Position EA as the acquisition accelerator and Boost as a competitive edge — they enhance OEM Appraisal, not replace it.
"OEM Appraisal ensures compliance. Expert Appraisal and Boost help you acquire more vehicles."
⚠
Stay in the OEM Lane
Compliance
Kia dealers included in the OEM program should not be retail-contracted outside the OEM process. Enrollment must align with Kia OEM communications and Shift Digital workflows. Improper enrollment = delays, re-contracting, BTS remediation.
"To avoid delays, Kia dealers should always enroll through the OEM path when notified."

Properly enrolled Kia dealers get: Kia-approved KBB trade-in experience, OEM-aligned branding, optional EA and Dealer Boost upgrades, and structured onboarding & training support. Frame compliance as what unlocks the full value stack.
