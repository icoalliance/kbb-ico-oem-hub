Seller Talking Points — Subaru
🎯
Lead Driver Is the Active OEM Product
Product Scope
Subaru Certified Digital currently supports Lead Driver as the active OEM-certified KBB tool. It's the right product to lead with for Subaru retailers today. Keep the conversation focused here — do not reference future products to dealers.
"Today, Lead Driver is the OEM-certified KBB solution available to Subaru retailers."
💵
Co-Op Varies — Always Confirm the Rate
Co-Op
Subaru co-op is 50–70% depending on the dealer — not a flat rate. Always confirm the specific dealer's co-op percentage before quoting. Contact Zach Cowen or check the Subaru portal for the dealer's rate. Never assume 70%.
"Co-op rates vary by dealer — let me confirm your specific rate before we talk numbers."
⚡
Website Provider Determines Price & Co-Op
Pricing
$179/mo with co-op for dealers on an approved provider (Dealer.com, Dealer eProcess, Dealer Inspire, Dealer On, Team Velocity). $200/mo, no co-op via CAI for any other provider. Always check the dealer's website platform first.
"The price and co-op eligibility both depend on your website provider — let me check that first."
⚠
Enroll Through the Portal — Not Retail
Compliance
Subaru retailers must enroll through Subaru Certified Digital — not a retail KBB contract. Retail enrollment is discouraged for OEM-eligible dealers and can result in delayed activation, re-contracting, and compliance issues.
"Using the Subaru Certified Digital path ensures compliance and avoids re-work."
🏛
Dealer-Paid — No Free Baseline
Model
Subaru does not offer a free OEM Appraisal baseline. Dealers choose and pay for Lead Driver through the certified portal. The co-op story (50–70%) significantly reduces the net cost — use this to anchor the value conversation.
📈
Subaru Is Actively Expanding the Program
Program Direction
OEM program updates indicate Subaru is actively prioritizing expansion of KBB products within its certified ecosystem. Getting dealers enrolled in Lead Driver now positions them for a smooth transition when additional products become available.
"Subaru retailers enroll in KBB trade-in tools through Subaru's OEM-certified digital program."
