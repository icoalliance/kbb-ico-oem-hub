Seller Talking Points — Lexus
🥇
Lexus Follows the Toyota/Lexus OEM Framework
Eligibility
Lexus is OEM-certified under the Toyota/Lexus framework via Shift Digital. Enrollment follows the same OEM process as Toyota — coordinated through Toyota/Lexus OEM communications. Retail contracting is not permitted once a dealer is eligible.
"Lexus participates in an OEM-certified KBB program, so enrollment must follow the Lexus/Toyota OEM path."
⚡
Existing KBB Dealers Activate Fast
Enrollment
Dealers already using KBB ICO get OEM Appraisal added at $0 — no new retail contract, no lengthy onboarding. Shortened training, minimal disruption. This is the fast-track for dealers already in the KBB ecosystem.
"If you're already using KBB, activation is simple. If not, we'll guide you through contracting and training."
🏠
Buying Center Is Lexus-Exclusive
Unique Upgrade
Unlike other OEM programs, Lexus supports a Buying Center upgrade — a Lexus-exclusive capability alongside Expert Appraisal. This is a differentiator in the Toyota/Lexus framework. Lead with it for dealers who want maximum acquisition capability.
"OEM Appraisal is the foundation; Expert Appraisal and Buying Center help you acquire more vehicles."
⚠
Compliance Is Actively Audited
Compliance
Toyota/Lexus actively manages OEM enrollment lists and audits SKU usage. Retail signing outside the OEM path is a breach. Improper enrollment results in delayed activation, re-contracting, and BTS remediation.
"To avoid delays or rework, Lexus dealers should always enroll through the OEM path."
🏛
Premium Brand — Lead With KBB Trust
Value Proposition
Lexus buyers are among the most brand-conscious consumers in automotive. KBB's #1 most-trusted status carries real weight here. The OEM-aligned branding and compliant valuation experience reinforce the Lexus premium positioning.
✔
Full Value Stack When Enrolled
Value Add
Properly enrolled Lexus dealers get: Lexus-approved KBB trade-in experience, OEM-aligned branding and compliance, optional EA and Buying Center upgrades, and structured onboarding with OEM-supported training.
