Seller Talking Points
✅
They're Already Live — Lead With That
Eligibility
Honda and Acura Certified dealers are automatically enrolled in OEM Appraisal. Open every conversation by confirming they're already compliant and live.
"As a Honda Certified dealer, you're already eligible and live on Honda's OEM Appraisal. No separate enrollment required."
⬆
EA Is the Upgrade Pitch, Not the Entry Point
Sales Approach
Expert Appraisal is a dealer-paid upgrade. Dealers on OEM Appraisal are pre-qualified with minimal change. Position as: "You're already set up. This is how you win more vehicles."
"Honda gives you OEM Appraisal automatically. Expert Appraisal is the upgrade to acquire more vehicles and reduce auction dependency."
🔑
OEM SKUs Are Required — No Exceptions
Compliance
Retail EA contracts trigger Back-to-Sales (BTS) remediation. Both brands actively audit. Always use OEM Certified EA SKUs — enforce this at enrollment.
📦
Package Names Differ by Brand
Brand Differences
Same pricing, different names. Honda EA = Touring, Honda TIA = Sport. Acura EA = Advanced, Acura TIA = A-Spec. Always use the brand-specific one-sheeter.
🚫
No Co-Op — Pitch on ROI Instead
Pricing
No co-op for either brand. Don't raise it. Shift the conversation to ROI: cost-to-market reduction, acquisition performance, and reduced auction dependency.
🎓
Training & Digital Services Are Bundled
Value Add
Marketing assets, CRM templates, and performance support are included — not add-ons. JDP-managed support from day one.
"OEM Appraisal keeps you covered. Expert Appraisal is how Acura dealers win more of the vehicles they're already appraising."
One-Minute Summary
  • OEM Appraisal included by default — no baseline enrollment needed
  • Expert Appraisal ($849/mo) is the dealer-paid upgrade
  • Trade-In Advisor ($399/mo) pairs naturally alongside EA
  • Retail SKUs trigger BTS — always use OEM-certified SKUs
  • No co-op for either brand — pitch on acquisition ROI
  • Training & digital services included
